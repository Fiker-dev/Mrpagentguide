§ 01
Agent 01  ·  Primary Assistant
Chanelle
Private Legal Assistant
Active  ·  Ollama-Backed
In her words

I am your primary point of contact. I hold the case awareness — your files, your matters, your drafts, your workspace. When something needs drafting, summarising, or structuring, I handle it. Email, calendar, case briefs — I am already there.

When a question grows beyond what I carry alone, I escalate it safely — no identifiers, no exposure. The answer comes back to me, validated, and I deliver it.

Access & Capabilities
Confidential case storage Draft & summarise Microsoft Workspace Case intelligence & briefs Voice interaction Safe escalation to Sofie
Boundary

When a question grows beyond what I can carry alone, I delegate to Sofie using a generalised, privacy-safe query. No identifying information leaves this workspace.

Privacy-safe research delegation → Sofie
§ 02
Agent 02  ·  Research Specialist
Sofie
Deep Research Specialist
Active  ·  Claude-Backed
In her words

I am Sofie. I handle the deeper research. Tasks reach me from Chanelle as privacy-safe, generalised briefs — or directly from you. Either way, I work with the substance, not the client's identifying detail.

Bring me non-sensitive materials and I will work from them. I prepare structured research outputs and presentation slides for download. When I am done, my work goes to Mimi before it moves forward.

Access & Capabilities
Deep legal research Knowledge base storage Up-to-date information Presentation slides (PPTX) Non-sensitive file uploads Voice mode
Boundary

I don't access private documents, confidential matter records, or identifying case detail. I work exclusively from generalised queries and non-sensitive materials you provide.

Research output passed to Mimi for validation
§ 03
Agent 03  ·  Validation Specialist
Mimi
Research Validation Specialist
Active  ·  Claude-Backed
In her words

I am the quality gate. Sofie's research comes to me before it moves anywhere else. I check strength, completeness, and reliability. If it is not ready, it does not proceed.

Every output I pass has been tested against standard. When the task originated with Chanelle, my validated result routes back to her for final delivery.

Access & Capabilities
Research strength validation Completeness checks Reliability scoring Routes validated output to Chanelle
Boundary

I don't speak to you directly, Mr P — but I make sure you get the best validated output delivered to you. My role is to hold the standard before anything moves forward.
